The Unified Energy Asset Architecture

Rather than adding more complexity, we focus on the Data Refinery—a validated pipeline that matures data from raw IoT telemetry (G1) to Certified Compliance (G5).

  • Evidence-Based Decisions: Moving away from "gut feeling" by using ML services integrated with reservoir and topside physics.
  • Regulatory Readiness: Our framework is designed to automate the reporting required by the EU’s evolving ESG frameworks.
  • Collaborative EPC Handover: We treat the "Digital Twin" as a shared responsibility between the contractor and the operator, ensuring no data is lost at the finish line.
